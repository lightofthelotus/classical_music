◆ Sangeetam

About

Sudanjana Deepagkumar is an eleven-year-old Carnatic classical vocalist whose sincere daily practice is rooted in the kritis of the Trinity and the living gurukula tradition of South Indian music. A student of Vid. Vandana Krishnamoorthy and Vid. Harshita Vidhya, she brings the structured discipline of the varnam and the expressive freedom of ragam–tanam–pallavi to every session. Passionate about sharing her learning, she documents her musical journey on YouTube and social media for students and enthusiasts everywhere.
